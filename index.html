Hello! I’m Veronica,

I’m a UX Designer & Graphic Designer based in Norcal. Outside of my career I love making matcha lattes and playing the bass & synthesizer in an indie rock band. Currently I am enrolled as a UX student at UC Berkeley and I’m always ready to expand my knowledge in the world of digital design.

My UX Skills

User Research

Conducting user research is a crucial part of my process. I begin by defining clear research objectives and questions to guide my efforts. I then employ a variety of methods such as user interviews, surveys, and usability testing to gain insights into user needs and behaviors, ensuring that my design solutions are tailored to deliver a seamless and user-centric experience.

User Interface Design

I craft intuitive and visually appealing interfaces, focusing on clear information architecture, user-friendly navigation, and a cohesive visual design that aligns with the brand's identity. My design process includes wireframing, prototyping, and iterative testing to ensure the final interface delivers an exceptional user experience.

Collaborating & Teamwork

I actively engage with cross-functional teams by fostering open communication and empathy. I value diverse perspectives, encourage feedback, and maintain a user-centric mindset throughout the design process, ensuring that everyone's input contributes to creating effective and user-friendly solutions.
